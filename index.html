About

Fernando Yakobo — visual storyteller from Lusaka.

Fern Media is the studio of Fernando Yakobo, a Zambian photographer. He takes pictures for portraits, brands, and magazines.His style uses natural light and simple, careful designs. He works with creators, business owners, weddings, events, and luxury brands.

His goal is to tell your story well and make beautiful images that look great for many years.

Every photograph should tell a story, create emotion, and leave a lasting impression.
