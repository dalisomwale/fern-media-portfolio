About Fern Media

Behind every frame,
a deliberate eye.

Fern Media is the studio of Fernando Yakobo, a Lusaka-based photographer building a body of work around honesty in front of the lens — portraits, brand stories, and live moments shot the way they actually happened.

What started as a personal pursuit has grown into a working studio trusted by entrepreneurs, executives, and couples across Zambia. The approach stays the same at every scale: understand the subject first, then let the camera do quiet work.
